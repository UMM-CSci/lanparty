What is it?

The LAN (Local Area Network) Party is a generally biannual event organized by the Computer Science Club. The purpose is to meet other gamers, play new and favorite games, and participate in organized games and tournaments. We welcome you to bring a desktop, laptop, and/or game console.

Power & Networking

We will do our best to provide two 15 amp style power outlets per person. Wired network connections will be available in great abundance. Wireless networking is also available, but will not be on the same network as the provided wired networks. The wireless networking equipment may struggle with the number of people in attendance, and we cannot guarantee that it will perform well.
